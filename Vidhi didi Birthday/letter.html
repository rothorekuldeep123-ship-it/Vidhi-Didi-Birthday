Dear Vidhi Didi,

When we first met, I didn’t really understand you. You felt quiet, a little self-centered, egoistic at times, and mostly lost in your own world. That was my first impression, and honestly, I never thought it would change the way it did.

But time has a way of revealing people slowly. Through conversations, shared moments, and even long silences, we began to understand each other better. Without realizing it, our bond grew deeper and more emotional.

There came a moment in my life when I needed guidance more than anything. I asked you for advice about something only the two of us knew. I still remember how carefully you listened, how calmly you understood, and how thoughtfully you guided me.

In that moment, you didn’t feel like just someone giving advice. You felt like a real elder sister — someone who genuinely cared, someone who wanted the best for me.

There are things I can’t share with my real sister, things I keep locked inside. But with you, I could open up without fear, without hesitation, and without being judged.

That is what makes you so special to me. You are not just a part of my life, you are the elder sister I found along the way.

— Always grateful
